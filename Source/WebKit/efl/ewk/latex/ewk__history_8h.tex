\subsection{Detailed Description}
The history (back-\/forward list) associated with a given ewk\+\_\+view. 

Changing the history affects immediately the view, changing the current uri, for example.

When ewk\+\_\+view is navigated or uris are set, history automatically updates. That's why no direct access to history structure is allowed.
